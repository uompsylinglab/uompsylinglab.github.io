About Us

We are a group of psycholinguists in the Department of Linguistics and English Language at The University of Manchester. We are primarily interested in written sentence processing - that is, the way in which written sentences are understood. Our work focuses predominantly on online semantic processing - the way in which meaning is derived on a word-by-word or phrase-by-phrase basis from written texts.

Our Lab Director is Vera Hohaus. Our Lab Manager is Paul Stott.

If you'd like to get in touch to discuss our research or to discuss how we might support you in your own research, please contact Paul Stott (Lab Manager) using the Contact Form provided.
