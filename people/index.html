people

Posted Feb 20, 2020

The director of the lab is Dr Vera Hohaus. She is supported with the running of the lab by the Lab Assistant, Alina. The lab is regularly used by researchers at all levels in the department of English Language and Linguistics, be that undergraduate and MA students working on dissertation projects, or PhD students and lecturers conducting research. Some of the ongoing research in the lab is also part of a collaboration with colleagues in Tübingen, Germany.
